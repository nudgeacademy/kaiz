The Island Chef

Our grandfather, a master chef from the Andaman & Nicobar Islands, perfected a secret slow-cooking recipe while running the kitchen at the historic Bismilla Hotel—the most popular establishment of its time.

The Father's Vision

Passed down to our father, the recipe became a home-based culinary tradition for local gatherings. As demand grew, they rented massive cooking vessels, paving the way for a proper event catering management firm and restaurants.

The Legacy Today

Today, we proudly carry this three-generation journey forward. Kaiz Kitchen has evolved into a premier event catering enterprise and three thriving restaurants, still following the exact island recipe brought by our grandfather.

"The aromatic flavored biriyani which can take you back to a memorable moment."

At Kaiz Kitchen, we preserve a rich generational heritage. Our story spans from the iconic Bismilla Hotel in the Andaman & Nicobar Islands, through our father's event catering breakthroughs, and finally to our three signature restaurants. We are still following the original recipe brought by our grandfather, keeping the traditional slow-cooking style alive.
